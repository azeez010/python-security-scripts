 - Boil 6 cups of water in a saucepan
 - Add about 300g of macaroni pasta
 - Add about 0.5g of dried shredded basil leaves
 - Cook for 7 to 8 minutes until tender while stirring occasionally
 - Drain the left over water using a sieve
 - Return cooked macaroni to saucepan
 - Add 1.5 tablespoon of non-hydrogenated margarine
 - Add 0.5 cup of skimmed milk
 - Add cheese sauce mix: dried whey from milk, cheddar cheese, pinch of salt with butter
 - Mix well and allow to cool
 - It makes 6 servings of 3/4 cup each
